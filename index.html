Biography

Zijian Zhang is currently a Professor in the School of Cyberspace Science and Technology at Beijing Institute of Technology, China. His current research interests include AI Security, Blockchain Security, Data Privacy and Anonymous Communication. He has published more than 100 research papers in international peer-reviewed conferences, transactions, and magazines, including ICML, WWW, AAAI, INFOCOM, TDSC, TIFS, TKDE, TMC, TPDS, etc. He has won the best paper award on IWQoS'17, ICC'24 and ICA3PP'20.
