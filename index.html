Biography

Zijian Zhang (张子剑) is currently a Professor in the School of Cyberspace Science and Technology at Beijing Institute of Technology, China.

His current research interests include Anonymous Communication, Blockchain-applied Analysis, LLM security and Data Privacy. He has published more than 100 research papers in international peer-reviewed conferences, transactions, and magazines, including ICML, WWW, AAAI, INFOCOM, ACL, TKDE, TOSEM, TPDS, TSE, TMC, etc. He has won the best paper award on IWQoS'17, ICA3PP'20 and ICC'24.
